# MoonGoons Living Hideout Rebuild

This branch replaces the flat precinct card board with a 3D lunar hideout/city view.

## Implemented player-facing result

- Full cutaway precinct with connected corridors, lunar terrain, beacon tower, landing pad, and patrol shuttle
- Eight room modules with room-specific equipment, lighting, damage states, labels, doors, and job positions
- Clickable rooms with repair, upgrade, assignment, and camera-focus actions
- Autonomous MoonGoons personnel walking between rooms and working at stations
- Visible alien, hybrid, and human Authority characters with division armor and rank details
- Camera pan, orbit, zoom, room focus, mouse controls, keyboard controls, and touch dragging
- Officer training, healing, and room posting windows
- Patrol call selection, three-officer formation, dispatch, tactical battle, arrests, and recovery
- Custody processing, interrogation, transfer, chapter tasks, daily tasks, and rewards
- Generated precinct ambience, UI sounds, alerts, repair effects, upgrade tones, work sounds, and dispatch audio
- Existing management, save/load, progression, web, Android, and RTS systems preserved

The uploaded Police Chief APK is used only as a structural reference. All code, geometry, characters, names, art direction, balance, and content remain original MoonGoons work.
