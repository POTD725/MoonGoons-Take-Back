# MoonGoons Living Hideout Rebuild

This branch replaces the flat precinct card board with a 3D lunar hideout/city view.

## Required player-facing result

- Full cutaway precinct with connected corridors and exterior pads
- Eight room modules with room-specific equipment and lighting
- Clickable rooms with repair, upgrade, assignment, and management actions
- Autonomous MoonGoons personnel walking between rooms and working at stations
- Patrol crews entering and leaving through the transfer/hangar side
- Camera pan, zoom, focus, and room selection
- Existing management, patrol, custody, task, save, and combat systems preserved

The uploaded Police Chief APK is used only as a structural reference. All code, geometry, characters, names, art direction, balance, and content remain original MoonGoons work.
