# Living Precinct Camera Controls

The living 3D precinct supports all of the following control methods:

- Arrow keys or WASD: pan across the station
- Left mouse drag: pan the station view
- Right mouse drag: orbit the camera
- Mouse wheel: zoom in and out
- Q and E: rotate left and right
- Plus and minus: zoom
- Home or F: center and reset the camera
- Touch drag: pan on phones and tablets
- Trackpad pan and magnify gestures: pan and zoom
- On-screen camera panel: pan, rotate, zoom, and reset without a keyboard

A quick click or tap without dragging still selects a room.
