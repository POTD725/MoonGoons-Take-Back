# Phase Eight: Syndicate Economy and Doctrine Guide

## What changed

The Syndicate now has a live faction economy called the **War Chest**. It grows continuously from the Hideout and grows faster while Siphon Arrays are active.

The War Chest unlocks doctrines in order. These doctrines change the enemy roster and raid pressure during the match.

## War Chest counterplay

Peacekeepers do not need to wait for the Hideout to attack.

- Destroy an active **Siphon Array** to cut the Syndicate War Chest by 24.
- Secure a territory sector to cut the War Chest by 18.
- Build Forward Relays, maintain patrol routes, and use Tactical Scan to locate the threats that feed enemy funding.

A sector capture now does more than grant your side income. It also slows enemy escalation.

## Doctrine one: Ghost Protocol

**Unlock threshold:** 65 War Chest.

Some new Runners become **Shades**.

- Shades are fast infiltrators.
- They have lower integrity than Bruisers but move much faster than standard Runners.
- Visible Shades receive a violet detection marker.
- Counter Shades with Patrol Deputy vision, Forward Relay coverage, Tactical Scan, and focused fire.

## Doctrine two: Black Market Forge

**Unlock threshold:** 155 War Chest.

New Bruisers receive better plating and heavier breach charges.

- Armored Bruisers have 28% more integrity.
- Their damage is increased by 25%.
- Counter them with Riot Vanguard Shield Wall, Security Turret chokepoints, and concentrated Deputy fire.

## Doctrine three: Relay Network

**Unlock threshold:** 280 War Chest.

The Syndicate accelerates its attack routing.

- Future attack waves are scheduled faster.
- The wave timer cannot fall below five seconds through this doctrine.
- Treat unhandled Siphon alerts as a strategic emergency before this doctrine activates.

## Enemy roster

| Unit or asset | Role | Practical response |
|---|---|---|
| Runner | Fast raider | Patrol Deputies, Turrets, open-field kiting |
| Shade | Infiltrator | Forward Relay sight, Tactical Scan, focus fire |
| Bruiser | Breach unit | Shield Wall, choke Turrets, combined fire |
| Siphon Array | Economic sabotage | Patrol routes and Tactical Scan |

## Status panel

A violet **Syndicate War Chest** panel appears near the northern battlefield edge. It reports:

- Current War Chest value
- Currently active doctrine
- Next doctrine and its unlock value

This is the first live Syndicate faction slice. A fully player-selectable Syndicate base, construction menu, and independent worker economy remains a later Phase Eight continuation after the AI economy is stable and verified.
